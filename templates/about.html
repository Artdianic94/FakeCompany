About FakeCompany

A fictional technology company built for cybersecurity education.

Our mission

FakeCompany simulates a realistic corporate intranet used in red-team penetration testing exercises. The portal provides HR workflows, employee directories, and administrative tools — intentionally containing security weaknesses for hands-on learning.

Portal features

  • Role-based employee portal (guest, employee, admin)
  • Internal feedback and communications channel
  • HR record management with confidential personnel data
  • Administrator user search and account settings
